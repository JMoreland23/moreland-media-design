faqs:
- title: How much does a new website cost?
  answer: The cost of a new website varies based on your specific requirements, including design complexity, number of pages, and desired functionalities. We provide customized quotes tailored to your needs. For a detailed assessment, please contact us directly.

- title: How long will it take to get a new website?
  answer: The timeline for developing a new website typically ranges from 3 to 5 weeks. This duration can vary depending on project complexity, the availability of content, and timely feedback. We'll work closely with you to establish a timeline that aligns with your goals.

- title: Do you only create WordPress websites?
  answer: While WordPress is a popular platform we specialize in due to its flexibility and user-friendliness, we also develop websites using other platforms like Next.js, depending on the project's requirements. Our goal is to choose the most suitable platform to meet your specific needs.

- title: Will my website be mobile-friendly?
  answer: Absolutely. We prioritize responsive design to ensure your website functions seamlessly across all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. This approach enhances user experience and is favored by search engines.

- title: Will you maintain my site for me?
  answer: Yes, we offer ongoing website maintenance services to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. Our maintenance packages can be tailored to your specific needs.

- title: Do I have to be local to work with you?
  answer: No, we work with clients from various locations. Communication is facilitated through email, phone calls, and video conferences, allowing us to collaborate effectively regardless of your geographical location.

- title: What is a Content Management System (CMS)?
  answer: A Content Management System (CMS) is a platform that allows you to manage and update your website's content without needing extensive technical knowledge. We often use WordPress as our CMS of choice due to its user-friendly interface and versatility.

- title: Can I update the website myself once it's built?
  answer: Yes, after your website is launched, you'll have full access to the CMS, enabling you to make updates and changes as needed. We also provide training and support to help you become comfortable with managing your site.

- title: What if I need help, do you offer support?
  answer: Certainly. We offer various support options, including ongoing maintenance plans and ad-hoc assistance, to ensure your website continues to operate smoothly. Our team is readily available to address any issues or questions you may have.

- title: How do I get started with my new website?
  answer: To begin, please contact us to discuss your project in detail. We'll assess your needs, provide a customized proposal, and outline the next steps to bring your vision to life.
